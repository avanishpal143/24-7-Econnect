Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our communication services

The WhatsApp Business API is designed for medium and large businesses to communicate with customers at scale. Unlike the regular WhatsApp app, the API allows automated messaging, chatbots, bulk broadcasts, CRM integrations, and multi-agent support — all through a verified business account with a Green Tick.

We deliver OTP SMS within 5 seconds via dedicated priority routes with 99.99% uptime. Our ISO-certified APIs connect directly to multiple telecom operators, ensuring the fastest possible delivery with automatic retrial mechanisms for failed messages.

DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology) registration is mandatory by TRAI for all businesses sending commercial SMS in India. Without it, your messages will be blocked. We provide complete DLT onboarding support — from entity registration to template approval — making the process hassle-free for you.

Absolutely. We support one-click integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, and more. Our REST APIs are built for all programming languages, enabling you to automate order confirmations, abandoned cart recovery, appointment reminders, and customer engagement workflows.

Google RCS is the next evolution of SMS — it supports high-res images, carousels, interactive buttons, and verified sender branding, all delivered to the native messaging app. If you want richer engagement than plain SMS without requiring customers to download an app, RCS is the ideal choice.
